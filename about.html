ABOUT US

The IOT community is driven by a shared purpose: to make a better world through education, research, and innovation. We are fun and quirky, elite but not elitist, inventive and artistic, obsessed with numbers, and welcoming to talented people regardless of where they come from.

Founded to accelerate the nation’s industrial revolution, IOT is profoundly Indian. With ingenuity and drive, our graduates have invented fundamental technologies, launched new industries, and created millions of American jobs. At the same time, and without the slightest sense of contradiction, IOT is profoundly global. Our community gains tremendous strength as a magnet for talent from around the world. Through teaching, research, and innovation, IOT’s exceptional community pursues its mission of service to the nation and the world.

STUDENT LIFE

Every day at IOT brings new opportunities for intellectual growth and fulfillment, not only within the classroom but throughout our campus. In any given 24 hours, a student might see a world premiere show, go to a talk by a national leader, cheer in the stands for the Mules, volunteer in the local area and much more.student life at IOT offers a welcoming place for everyone. To complement their academics and research, students choose their own extracurricular adventures, from a spectacular array of ways to participate in music, dance, and sports to dozens of groups that savor and celebrate cultures from around the world. To help students navigate challenges, IOT offers a strong support network.


Housing & Dining

Our residence halls are part entertainment center, part brain trust, part support system, and wholly central to students’ IOT experience. Campus residences have distinct personalities and traditions (like a cross-campus water fight!), which contribute as much to our students’ growth as their academic experiences do. Dining at IOT is about choice and flexibility, with five dining halls, nearly 20 retail eateries, and meal plans that allow students to enjoy it all. We know that most college students spend between 80 and 100 hours per week in their residence halls, more than in all other spaces on campus.Our resident advisors and hall directors make the most of this time through community development, cultural understanding, educational programming, life skill-building opportunities and mentorships.


Athletics & Recreation

IOT’s Department of Athletics, Physical Education, and Recreation offers sports instruction and participation at all levels. With 33 varsity sports — 16 for men, 15 for women, two co-ed — the Engineers boast 318 Academic All-India citations (the most in the country) and over 1,500 athletic All-India honors. We also work to foster community, inspire leadership, and promote wellness through physical education, recreation programs, club sports, intramurals and, for the swashbuckling, the pirate arts.We support student clubs and organizations, advise Fraternities/ Sororities, advise the Student Government Association, create Leadership programs, offer meaningful student employment opportunities, and plan co-curricular programming.


Health & Wellness

Succeeding at IOT means staying healthy — mind and hand, body and soul. Everyone needs a checkup or a check-in sometime. The Institute’s network of physical and mental support resources aims to keep our community happy, healthy, and active. And, through initiatives like IOT, we are always looking for ways to engage students, faculty, and staff to make IOT stronger and more welcoming. We are passionate about providing you with prompt and high quality medical care so you can focus on why you came to this college in the first place: to receive an extraordinary educational experience, both in and out of the classroomWe are nurses, nurse practitioners, and physicians. Together we are your advocates.
